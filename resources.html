Common Questions

Q: Where can I download the datasets?
A: After clicking the "Challenge" tab above, follow the links to the relevant challenge site on Codalab. There, you can register for the challenges and download the datasets.

Q: I did not take part in any challenge, can I still attend DeepGlobe workshop at CVPR?
A: Absolutely yes.

Q: Do I need to submit a paper to participate in the challenge?
A: Yes. To be considered for the prizes, you must submit your short paper (3 pages + 1 page for references) that explains your approach.

Q: I do not want to participate in the challenge, can I still submit a paper?
A: We would love to, however this is not an open call for papers. We would like to structure the workshop around the challenge, so this year DeepGlobe will only accept papers that compete in one of the challenges.

Q: Can I use other open datasets to train my models?
A: Only if the datasets are open and free, and they are properly referred in your paper, then you can use other datasets. We encourage sharing those resources in the forum of the relevant challenge.

Q: Can I use DeepGlobe datasets for other purposes?
A: Unfortunately not for now. By accepting the terms and conditions, the participant agrees that the dataset is currently only released for this challenge. We are planning to release it with an open license, however this is not the case for now.

Q: I have technical questions, should I e-mail the committee?
A: We highly encourage you to check the forums of the challenges first. Most of the technical questions are already answered there. You can also discuss with your peers in the forum.

Q: What is the amount of prizes?
A: We are still communicating with sponsors, stay tuned.
